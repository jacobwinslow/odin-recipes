Pizza

Description

A classic Italian pizza featuring a thin crust topped with bright tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, and fragrant basil. Simple, fresh, and delicious.

Ingredients

Ingredients for the dough
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
Ingredients for the Topping
  • ½ cup tomato sauce (or crushed tomatoes)
  • 1–2 cloves garlic, minced (optional)
  • 6–8 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1–2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. the dough: Mix flour, yeast, and salt. Add warm water and olive oil, then mix until a dough forms. Knead for 5–7 minutes. Cover and let rise for 1 hour (or use ready-made dough).
  2. the oven: Heat to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it inside to heat.
  3. the dough: On a floured surface, stretch the dough into a thin round.
  4. Add the toppings: Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough. Add garlic if using. Arrange mozzarella slices on top. Drizzle with a bit of olive oil and season lightly with salt and pepper.
  5. Bake: Transfer to a baking sheet or hot stone and bake 10–12 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  6. Finish: Add fresh basil leaves and an optional drizzle of olive oil. Slice and enjoy!
