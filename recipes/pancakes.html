Pancakes

Description

Light, soft, and easy to make—perfect for breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter (plus more for the pan)
  • Optional: vanilla extract, fresh fruit, or chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients: In a bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Add wet ingredients: Add milk, egg, and melted butter. Stir gently until just combined—small lumps are okay.
  3. Heat the pan: Warm a non-stick pan over medium heat and add a little butter.
  4. Cook the pancakes: Pour ¼ cup batter for each pancake. Cook until bubbles appear on top, then flip and cook 1–2 minutes more.
  5. Serve: Enjoy warm with syrup, butter, or your favorite toppings.
