Fried Egg

Description

A quick and easy way to make a perfectly fried egg with crisp edges and a runny or set yolk—your choice.

Ingredients

  • 1–2 large eggs
  • 1–2 tsp butter or oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  2. Add the egg: Crack the egg into the pan gently to keep the yolk intact.
  3. Cook:
    • Sunny-side up: Cook 2–3 minutes until whites are set but yolk is still runny.
    • Over-easy: Flip carefully and cook 10–20 seconds.
    • Over-medium: Flip and cook 30–45 seconds.
    • Over-hard: Flip and cook 1–2 minutes until yolk is fully set.
  4. Season and serve: Sprinkle salt and pepper on top and enjoy with toast or any breakfast plate.
